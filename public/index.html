Spotify Playlist Saver

About

As someone who has spent lots of time making playlists, I would be crushed if something ever happened to Spotify and I no longer had access to my playlists. Although it's unlikely to happen, I made this tool so I can sleep at night.

How It Works

The tool will scan through all of your playlists and write them to a file. Upon finishing, the file will be downloaded to your browser.

How to Use

To use the tool, you must login to Spotify, the button below will take you to their login portal.
