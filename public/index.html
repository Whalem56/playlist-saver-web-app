Spotify Playlist Saver

About

As someone who has spent a lot of time making playlists, I'd be crushed if something happened to Spotify and I no longer had access to my playlists. It's unlikely to happen, I know, but I made this tool anyway so I can sleep at night.

How It Works

The tool will scan through all of your playlists and write them to a file. Upon finishing, the file will be downloaded to your browser.

How to Use

To use the tool, you must login to Spotify, the button below will take you to their login portal. Once signed in, the application will begin scanning your playlists. Enjoy!
